The traveler costs values reported by RPAT are the total of the following components:

  • Gas cost: the fuel consumption multiplied by the gas cost parameter
  • Fuel tax: the fuel consumption multiplied by the fuel tax parameter
  • Parking costs: the parking costs paid under the travel demand management policies in effect for the scenario
  • VMT charge: VMT multiplied by the VMT charge in effect for the scenario The values reported are annual costs by place type and account for light vehicle travelers only.
