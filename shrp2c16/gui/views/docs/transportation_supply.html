This file contains the amount of transportation supply in the base year in terms of lane miles of freeways and arterial roadways and the revenue miles operating by the transit system in the region.

Freeway and Arterial are total lane miles for those functional classes in the region. These data can be derived FHWA’s Highway Statistics data. (http://www.fhwa.dot.gov/policy/ohim/hs05/roadway_extent.htm)

Bus and Rail are annual bus and rail revenue miles per capita for the region. These data can be derived from the National Transit Database. http://www.ntdprogram.gov/ntdprogram/data.htm), where the annual database contains a “service” table that has annual revenue mile data by mode for each transit operator.
